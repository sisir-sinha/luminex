About Luminex Electrical

Welcome to Luminex Electrical, Perth's premier destination for top-quality domestic electrical services. Equipped with close to a decade of experience, our Perth based team excels in reliability, professionalism, and efficiency.

We specialise in interior and exterior lighting, switchboard upgrades, safety switches, smoke alarms, EV charging stations, and much more. Serving all Perth metro areas, we are dedicated to ensuring your home's electrical systems are safe, efficient, and up-to-date.
